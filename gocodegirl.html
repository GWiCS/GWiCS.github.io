Workshops

Workshop A: Digital Detectives

A person's phone is a treasure trove of information. Phones know where you've been, who you know, and what you say. In this activity, we will demonstrate how to extract information off an Android device, then we will use that information to find evidence to help us solve a "crime"! This workshop does not require any prior coding experience. Get ready to catch some criminals!
